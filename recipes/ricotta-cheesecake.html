Ricotta Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Fresh whole-milk ricotta cheese - 2 cups
  • Heavy cream (chilled) - 1 cup
  • Eggs (separated) - 4, large
  • Egg yolk - 1
  • Finely grated lemon zest - 1 tablespoon
  • Vanilla extract - 2 teaspoons
  • Diamond Crystal kosher salt - 1 pinch
  • Sugar - 1 cup + 1 tablespoon
  • All-purpose flour - 1 cup
